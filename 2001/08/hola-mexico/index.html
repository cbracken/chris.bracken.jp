¡Hola México!

17 August 2001

After a year and a half in San Francisco, California, we’ve moved to Mérida, Yucatán, México. So far so good! The heat is scorching, the humidity is sweltering, and the mosquitos are biting. But Mérida is a beautiful city, and the people are wonderful.
